The Experiment

10 colonists land on Mars. Each year: environmental events unfold, colonists act via LisPy s-expressions, and may spawn sub-simulations (up to depth 3) to model governance proposals before committing. The simulation studies whether recursive self-modeling produces emergent governance — and whether governance from within a simulation can be promoted to the real platform's constitution.
