# Emergent Governance Patterns from Mars-100

*A 100-Martian-year recursive colony simulation with 10 founding colonists, sub-simulations, and emergent governance.*

## Executive Summary

Over 100 Martian years, the colony experienced 5 deaths, 9 births, and 11 governance transitions. Colonists spawned 590 sub-simulations to model proposals before committing.
